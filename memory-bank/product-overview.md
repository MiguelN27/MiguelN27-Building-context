# Product overview
## What is about?
 It's a financial dashboard webpage, presenting a year-level executive view of business performance through the current year, used mainly for monitoring revenue, spending, profit and margin trends. it uses:
    
- financial movements like income/outcome amounts
- Categories
- Dates
- B2B/B2C segments

## Main Target users
The main target users are operations, finance or business decision-makers who wants a quick health check without reading raw transacions

## Main features of the page
1. A top-level financial overview for the full year.
2. KPI cards for total income, total outcome, profit, and profit margin.
3. A monthly income-vs-outcome trend chart.
4. A monthly profit margin trend chart.

## Usage for frequent and Occasional users
### Frequent users
For frequent users, the product helps as a lightweight monitoring dashboard: they can repeatedly check whether income is keeping ahead of outcome, whether margin is drifting month to month, and whether the business is becoming less efficient over time

### Occasional users
For occasional users, it helps in snapshot situations like monthly reviews, stakeholder updates, investor conversations, or a quick “is the business healthy right now?” check.
